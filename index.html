WIE AFFINITY GROUP

With the mission to empower women and to inspire girls worldwide to choose a career composing engineering and science,IEEE has set up a global network of IEEE members and volunteers ,IEEE Women In Engineering (WIE).

MISSION

With the vibrant community of IEEE women and men together who use their stupendous calibre to innovate for the benefit of humanity;we aim to facilitate recruitment of women globally in all technical disciplines.

VISION

Considering the compulsive demand of this ever changing world,IEEE WIE commits itself to make women put up with struggles in technical career and to face the music.By organizing workshops at major technical conferences and assisting with the formation of new IEEE affinity groups,they add colour to thier wings.It also put up women in leadership governance thereby enhancing an eminent career advancement.

Our WIE Chapter

With the aim to lessen the gender gap persisting in industries through a focus -driven initiative and identifying struggles of young female engineers;IEEE SB RIT WIE affinity group at present plans to organise various workshops and managerial sessions to improve technical skills and to keep abreast of latest technical discoveries. The branch proved it worth as five of its members were chosen to receive a travel grant to attend IEE WIE ILS at Bangalore.Over and above another five became winner of hackathon conducted by WIE Learn Program by Kerala section.
